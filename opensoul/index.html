How It Works

Three steps to autonomous marketing

Set objectives, staff your agency with AI agents, and watch them execute. You approve strategy — they do the work.

Set the Objective

"Launch product X to 10K signups in 90 days." Give your agency a clear goal — they'll figure out how to get there.

2

Let Them Execute
